Bettle Dice Game

How to play!

Moral Lesson of the game

The lesson a person can learn from playing dice is: You don't have any control for things being in your favor when everything depends on chance. Your odds are actual ly against you in dice because instead of the chance being 1:1 as in other games, here they are 6:1.

  • Number of Players: Two!
  • Objective: To have the highest outcome!
  • Gameplay:
  • Players take turns to roll the dice
  • The starting player rolls the dice & conceals the result.
  • If the player's score is the same, it's a tie!
  • The player with the highest score at the end of the game win the game
